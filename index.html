Phishing Attempts

Beware of unsolicited calls or messages from individuals claiming to be from "Help Desk" or "Mac Hippo Tech Support" as they are likely scams. To contact us, please reach out directly through our official social media accounts or contact form. We will never contact you from any other source.

Scammers often pose as tech support or offer fake cryptocurrency rewards to gain access to your funds or personal information. Do not disclose your wallet seed phrases or account passwords to anyone. If you are uncertain, seek advice from an official source at Mac Hippo.

Avoid clicking on links from suspected scammers, and be cautious when using fake wallets, escrows, wallet connecting services, or other services that require you to share your personal information or wallet details.

We typically do not initiate direct contact with our users unless we are responding to a question you have asked. Be cautious of new accounts, accounts with no profile pictures, and accounts that do not actively participate in online discussions. Genuine Mac Hippo fans enjoy interacting with each other, sharing memes, and engaging in real conversations.

Safety Steps

If you think you've accidentally given scammers information that could be used to steal your funds, here's what to do.

  1. Change your social media passwords
  2. Disconnect your wallet from any wallet connecting services
  3. If possible, withdraw funds from that wallet and transfer to a new, secure wallet
  4. Keep your new details safe - write passwords down on paper and store them in person rather than keeping those details online
